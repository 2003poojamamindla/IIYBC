About IIYBC

The India International Youth Biodiversity Conference (IYBC) empowers young leaders to actively engage in biodiversity conservation, sustainable development, and environmental governance through dialogue, innovation, and collaboration.

The inaugural National Youth Biodiversity Conference (NYBC-2025), organized by the Telangana Biodiversity Board, was a landmark event where postgraduate delegates from across India adopted the â€œHyderabad Declaration.â€ This declaration served as a 9-point roadmap in which the youth pledged to protect biodiversity and requested institutional support for sustainable entrepreneurship.

While NYBC-2025 successfully strengthened awareness on biodiversity conservation, IYBC-2026 is strategically designed to emphasize economic empowerment of youth. Currently, Telangana and India produce thousands of graduates in Life Sciences (Botany, Zoology, Forestry, and allied disciplines) every year; however, a significant proportion of them face unemployment due to limited exposure to market-ready skills.

At the same time, the Green Economy is rapidly expanding. Emerging sectors such as Sustainable Agriculture, Bio-Resource Management, Ecological Data Analysis, and Nature-based Enterprises are growing, yet there is a critical shortage of trained professionals to meet industry demand. Although young graduates possess strong biological knowledge, they often lack the technical, practical, and entrepreneurial skills required to translate this knowledge into viable livelihood opportunities.
